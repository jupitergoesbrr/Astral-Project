📧 How to Contact Math is Fun

The Contact Page is Key: You can send them a message directly through their Contact Us page.

The Email Address: If you are having issues and need a reply, watch out for your mail filters! They sometimes get blocked, so make sure your email allows messages from funmath@gmail.com.

Who is behind it? The site is maintained by Rod Pierce and many contributors, and they totally welcome feedback on improvements.

Don't Ask for Private Help: Heads up—they don't sell products directly, so they won't ask for payment or personal address details. Don't fall for scams!

🎮 What to Do If a Game Doesn't Work

  • Click the Game Area: Sometimes, especially with browser games, the game just needs to know you are "active." Click inside the game area to wake it up.
  • Switch to HTML5 Versions: Look for links to "HTML5" versions of puzzles if the old ones aren't loading.
  • Restart the Scene: If the game is acting buggy, try pressing the Ctrl key to restart the current level.
  • Check Browser Issues: Try switching to a modern browser like Chrome or Firefox.
  • "Script Running Too Long": Complex math tasks can cause the browser to freeze. Just give it a moment.
  • Save Data Problems: Progress can sometimes fail on certain browsers or mobile devices like iPads.
